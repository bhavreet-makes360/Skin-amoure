Founded in Intention
Amouré Skin was born from a simple, profound belief: dermatology should be a sanctuary, not a transaction. A sacred space where science and compassion meet, where your skin is honored and your story is heard.
Founded by Dr. Geeta in the heart of Ludhiana, our practice is more than a clinic—it's a wellness journey. Three women, three unique strengths, one shared purpose: to honor your skin with honesty, expertise, and genuine care that transcends the clinical.
We don't follow trends. We follow science, intuition, and the timeless wisdom that true beauty emerges from balance. We don't offer quick fixes. We offer sustainable transformation, built on trust and tailored to your unique essence.
